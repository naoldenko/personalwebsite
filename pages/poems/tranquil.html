Naol Denko — Poems

Tranquil

Tranquil…


That's how it feels.

When I peel,

The shells cocooning me.


It leads me.


To feel the world,

To understand more,

To never lose hope.


Until the day comes…


That I need to leave.


I'll never know….


So, I continue to peel,

The shells that contain me.


To manifest and eventually be,


Tranquil.
