SSI

March 2025

Long-term planning is speculative.

It is good to think ahead, but changes are inevitable.

I've learned firsthand with my numerous, meticulously crafted plans that ended up in a metaphorical trash bin.

Adopting super-specific, immediate goals with a desired outcome is better.

By having those super-specific, immediate goals with a desired outcome, you'll be able to have more clarity on what you want to achieve.

Multiple SSI goals instead of lengthy, long-term plans will keep things running in motion, similar to ticking boxes off a to-do list.

Implementing SSI goals can make life seem less Waterfall-like and more malleable/Agile-like.

The utility of this is more freedom in approaching your life and having control without being chained to a long-term plan.

Obviously, context matters.

Yet, I believe this methodology of thinking will alleviate a lot of stress and ensure overall life satisfaction will improve.
