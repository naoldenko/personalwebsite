Social Media

March 2025

Social media brought access to a wide variety of stories from people you wouldn't hear from on a day-to-day basis that would've been untold if it wasn't for this channel.

A whole pot of various personalities that share their defeats and triumphs.

There are obviously detriments to social media (e.g., its addicting nature). However, it's eye-opening to hear stories or connect with others in this realm.

Powerful, but dangerous at the same time.

Be cautious but be open.
